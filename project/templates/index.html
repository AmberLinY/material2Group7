Design Brief

Electronic waste is a growing problem, with 43 million tons generated in 2016. With the number of mobile phones worldwide in 2017 being 4.77 billion (Statista,2017), a significant percentage of electronic waste is related to mobile phone disposal (Knapton, 2017) . This is indicative of a linear economy where waste is generated without recycle. Our group aims to facilitate a circular economy where there is complete recyclability and minimal waste is generated. Some obstacles would be the high cost of recycling electronic devices as compared to creating a new electronic device and the lack of electronic device components recycling options.
